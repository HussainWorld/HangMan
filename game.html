Hangman is a word-guessing game where the player tries to guess a hidden word within a given category (e.g., “Fruits” or “Animals”). The word is represented by blank spaces, each corresponding to a letter. Correct guesses reveal the letters in the word, while incorrect guesses count toward your 6 attempts. Win by guessing the word before running out of tries, or lose if you reach 6 wrong guesses.
